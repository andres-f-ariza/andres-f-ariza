Other AI applications of impact in the field of small businesses

Product placement Supply chain T-shirt demand forecasting
What if a front-of-store manager take pictures of what the store looks like and show it to an AI and have an AI recommend where to place products to improve sales. Can an AI recommend to a buyer whether they should pay 20 dollars per yard for a piece of fabric now, or if they should keep looking because they might be able to find it cheaper elsewhere? It is a very different project than pizzeria demand forecasting because it depends on the pizzeria sales data. On the other side, getting to know which t-shirt stamp will generate a substantial amount of sales, depends on trending memes on social media.
